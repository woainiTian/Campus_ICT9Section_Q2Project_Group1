Empowering Future Leaders

At Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center, each child is nurtured in the holistic method of Dr. Maria Montessori.

Seipsum Facit Persona

"Man Makes Himself"


At O.B Montessori Center, this motto captures the heart of our philosophy: Every child has the power to shape their own future. We believe that education is to guide them to discover who they are and who they can become. Our students are aided to grow into capable, compassionate individuals—ready to lead, adapt, and change the world.
